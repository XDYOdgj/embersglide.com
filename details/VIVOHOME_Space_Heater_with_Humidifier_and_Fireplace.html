VIVOHOME Space Heater with Flame Effect and Humidifier

The VIVOHOME heater creates a warm and inviting atmosphere with its realistic 3D flame design. The flickering logs and gentle glow add a touch of elegance to any space, whether you're winding down after a long day or setting the mood for a cozy evening.
