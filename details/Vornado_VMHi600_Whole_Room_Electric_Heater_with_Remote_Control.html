The Vornado VMHi600 Whole Room Electric Heater

Keep your space warm and comfortable with the Vornado VMHi600 Whole Room Electric Heater. This modern heating solution uses Vortex Heat Circulation technology to spread warm air evenly across your room, eliminating cold spots and creating a cozy environment. Unlike conventional heaters, it delivers gentle and consistent warmth, making it ideal for all-day use without causing discomfort.

The Vornado VMHi600 features a digital thermostat that lets you set and maintain your preferred temperature with precision. Its remote control allows you to adjust settings from anywhere in your home, ensuring convenience and flexibility. The 1500W power output provides rapid heating, while the fan-only function adds extra air circulation for a refreshing feel.

Safety is a top priority with this heater. The cool-touch stainless steel casing ensures that you can handle the unit safely even during operation. Additional peace of mind comes from tip-over protection and an automatic shut-off system, which help prevent accidents and ensure reliable performance. The 12-hour timer lets you schedule heating for optimal energy savings and personalized comfort.

Whether you're heating your living room, bedroom, or office, the Vornado VMHi600 is designed to deliver efficient, quiet, and long-lasting warmth. Its intelligent climate control automatically adjusts the heat output to maintain a steady temperature, providing you with seamless comfort. With a combination of modern features and reliable performance, this heater is a perfect choice for anyone seeking a safe and effective heating solution.
